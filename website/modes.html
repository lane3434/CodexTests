AI Operation Modes

MIMIC Mode

A predictive shadow cursor displays upcoming actions, giving you real-time insight as the assistant learns.

TRAIN Mode

ShadowAI records inputs to build or refine the local model for personalized automation.

I AM YOU Mode

The assistant takes control, making decisions based on your established patterns while showing its reasoning in real time.
